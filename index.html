PubLoft was productized content marketing for startups.

We closed our doors in January 2020, having produced hundreds of articles for dozens of startups. We grew from $0 – $24,000 MRR in just 7 months, earning pre-seed investment from Jason Calacanis’s LAUNCH Accelerator the following year.

Though we did many things well, we ultimately suffered from split focus by trying to pursue GigLoft before fully hiring ourselves out of PubLoft. We learned many important lessons about business—including how easily relationships can be damaged by financial stress. We’re incredibly grateful to all our customers, team members, and the LAUNCH team for believing in us along the way!

Today, Mat helps startup founders raise money with Seedscout, and Jérémy continues to offer content marketing consulting for startups—learn more & inquire if you’re looking for this.
